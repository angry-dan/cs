Hello

We are the 25th Medway Scout group and we've been scouting since 1935. We're based on the Davis Estate in Chatham and have members aged from 5 upwards. We're all about having an adventure. We're up for trying something new, exploring our limits and having fun whilst we do it. If that sounds like it might be for you then please get in touch.
